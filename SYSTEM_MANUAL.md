## What is RPAS?

RPAS is the official research support service of Aldersgate College Inc. It helps students, researchers, and faculty with data analysis, manuscript review, questionnaire validation, and research consultation. All service requests are tracked online through this system at `https://tyronelopez.github.io/rpas/`.

---

## User Roles

**Researcher** — student or faculty who submits requests, tracks progress, messages analyst
**Data Analyst** — RPAS staff who handles requests, updates status, uploads results
**Admin** — RPAS office manager — approves accounts, assigns analysts, manages all requests
**Adviser** _(coming soon)_ — faculty adviser linked to a researcher's submission

## Getting Started

**Step 1 — Sign in with Google**
Go to the RPAS website and click "Sign in with Google." Use your registered email.

**Step 2 — Wait for approval**
New accounts are Pending until the admin approves them. You will get a notification when approved.

**Step 3 — Complete your profile (Researchers)**
Before submitting, fill in your Level, Department, and Program under Edit Profile. A yellow warning shows if incomplete.

**Step 4 — Submit a request**
Click "New Request," choose your service, fill in the title and abstract, attach files if needed.

## How to Submit a Request

1. Log in as a Researcher
2. Complete your profile (Level, Department, Program)
3. Click **New Request**
4. Fill in: Service Type, Research Title, Abstract (100–150 words, include 5–8 keywords)
5. Attach files if needed (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, JPG, PNG — max 20MB)
6. Click **Submit Request**

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: My account says Pending. What do I do?**
Wait for admin approval. If it has been more than 2 working days, message the RPAS office through the system.

**Q: I cannot submit a request.**
Your profile is incomplete. Fill in Level, Department, and Program under Edit Profile first.

**Q: How long does processing take?**
Research Consultation: a few days. Data analysis services: 3–7 working days. You will be notified of any updates.

**Q: Can I have multiple active requests?**
Yes.

**Q: I submitted the wrong service type.**
Contact admin through the RPAS office message tab. If not yet assigned, it can be cancelled.

**Q: How do I attach files?**
During submission, click the upload area or drag and drop. Accepted: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, XLS, JPG, PNG. Max 20MB.

**Q: Who is my analyst?**
Once assigned, the analyst name appears on your request detail page.

**Q: I cannot find my analyst's message.**
Click on the request → scroll to the Messages section.

**Q: What is an abstract and why is it required?**
A 100–150 word summary of your study — purpose, methods, and 5–8 keywords at the end. It helps the analyst understand your study before starting.

**Q: My dashboard is blank or stuck loading.**
Try Ctrl+Shift+R (hard refresh). If still blank, sign out and back in. If still not working, message RPAS office.

## For Admins

- **Approve users:** Admin Dashboard → Pending Users → Approve or Reject
- **Assign analyst:** Any submitted request → Manage → select analyst → save
- **Update status:** Manage dialog → select status → add notes → save
- **Manage users:** Admin Dashboard → Users tab

---

## For Analysts

- **Your requests:** Dashboard shows all assigned to you with status and researcher name
- **Update a request:** Click request → update status → add notes
- **Upload results:** Request detail page → upload file → mark as result
- **Message researcher:** Request → Messages → type and send
